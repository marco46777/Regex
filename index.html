Regular expressions

Regular expressions (often abbreviated as regex or regexp) are a powerful and flexible method for describing patterns in text. They are sequences of characters that form a search pattern, which can be used to match, locate, validate, extract, replace, or manipulate strings within larger bodies of text or data. Regular expressions are widely used in programming, data analysis, and text processing because they allow complex operations to be performed with relatively concise syntax.
